What is Pneumonia?

Pneumonia is an inflammation in the airspaces of the lungs due to fluids or bacteria. It is one of the top 10 causes of death in the United States.

In Chest X-Rays (CXRs), pneumonia can be seen as an opacity, since the fluids/bacteria prevents the X-Ray light from passing through. However, analyzing large volumes of CXRs is time-consuming for medical professionals, and may lead to some inaccuracies.

Because of this, the Radiology Society of North America (RSNA) hosted a competition to find machine learning methods in order to detect pneumonia.
